Drainage + substrate platform

Lightweight layers built for healthier buildings.

GreenRoof combines capillary drainage mats, root-safe waterproofing, and mineral-rich lightweight substrate to balance water retention with fast runoff during heavy rain. The result is a roof that protects the building envelope while supporting long-term plant health.

  • Modular drainage trays reduce standing water and simplify maintenance.
  • Low-load substrate keeps structural impact predictable.
  • Sensor-ready layers support irrigation automation and reporting.
