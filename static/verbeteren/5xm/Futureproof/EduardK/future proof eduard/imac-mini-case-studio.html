the origin story

Why mcase?

It started with a pile of beautiful, discarded Macintosh cases found at a recycling center. These machines — once symbols of creativity and innovation — were being sent to the landfill.

The original Macintosh enclosure is iconic. The aluminum chassis, the elegant proportions, the attention to detail — it's a piece of design history. So we asked: what if we could keep the case, but upgrade the heart?

That's how mcase was born. We carefully disassemble old Macintosh enclosures, adapt them with custom mounts and I/O modules, and fit a modern Mac mini inside. The result is a machine that honors its past while running the latest Apple Silicon.

It's not just a product — it's a statement: great design should never be wasted.
