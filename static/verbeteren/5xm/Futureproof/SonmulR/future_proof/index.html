The RESOLE Shoe Game

Catch and Recycle!

Most shoes are glued together in one big piece. When just one part breaks, you have to throw the whole shoe in the trash. This creates a lot of plastic waste.

RESOLE is different. Our shoes are made of separate parts that safely snap together. If a part breaks, you just replace that one piece instead of buying a whole new shoe!

How to play: Move your collector left and right to catch the good shoe parts and stay away from the trash. Watch out—the higher your score, the faster the parts fall!

Points: 0
Shield Health: 100%
Use your Left & Right Arrow keys (or the A & D keys) to move back and forth.
